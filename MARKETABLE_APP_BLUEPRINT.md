# Reliable Shop Systems Hub: Marketable Application Blueprint

## Product Identity
- Product name: Reliable Shop Systems Hub
- Category: Auto-repair shop operating platform
- Deployment modes: Hosted and desktop/LAN hub
- Monetization: One-time perpetual license

## Core Value Proposition
Reliable Shop Systems Hub gives independent repair shops one platform to run the full operation: service intake, scheduling, technician workflow, inspections, estimates, invoicing, inventory, messaging, and business reporting.

## Customer Problem It Solves
- Too many disconnected tools for appointments, write-ups, inspections, and invoicing
- Subscription-heavy software that raises costs and limits ownership
- Slow, error-prone handoffs between front office and technicians
- Missing customer visibility and communication consistency

## Differentiators
- One-time license model
- Full operational scope (not just invoicing)
- Public booking and portal support included
- AI-assisted workflows integrated into the operations stack
- Desktop/LAN mode option for shops that prefer local control

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Independent automotive repair shops
- 3 to 50 staff
- Owner-operators and service managers replacing spreadsheets + fragmented tools
- Shops seeking predictable software cost and long-term ownership

## Offer Structure
- Main offer: Reliable Shop Systems Hub perpetual license
- Optional services:
  - Onboarding and data migration package
  - Workflow customization package
  - Priority support package

## Pricing Packaging (Starter Recommendation)
- Perpetual license: primary one-time price anchor
- Launch incentive: limited-time onboarding credit
- Optional support add-on: annual recurring support plan

## Feature Proof Points to Use in Sales
- Public booking endpoint with controlled rate limits and anti-abuse design
- Secure session/auth gates and role-aware route protection
- Stripe-integrated storefront and webhook processing for license commerce
- Inventory, estimates, invoices, inspections, and scheduling APIs in one backend
- Extensive automated tests in api-server/test for critical workflows

## Sales Motion
- Channel 1: Direct outreach to local independent repair shops
- Channel 2: Demo-led calls with owner + service writer workflows
- Channel 3: Content proof (before/after workflow videos)

## Demo Flow (15 minutes)
1. Show appointment booking from public page.
2. Convert booking to work order.
3. Add inspection photos + estimate.
4. Convert estimate to invoice and collect payment.
5. Show reporting and technician time/labor visibility.

## Required Launch Assets
- Installer package with product branding
- Product one-pager
- Sales demo script
- Pricing page and checkout flow
- Onboarding checklist
- Terms/privacy/support policies

## 30-Day Launch Plan
- Week 1: Final QA and first 3 pilot shops
- Week 2: Gather testimonials and tighten onboarding
- Week 3: Push local outreach and referral incentives
- Week 4: Publish case study and scale paid acquisition test

## Success Metrics
- Demo-to-close rate
- Time-to-onboard per shop
- Activation rate (first invoice created within 7 days)
- Retention proxy (active work orders and invoices per week)
