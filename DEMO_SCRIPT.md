# Reliable Shop Systems Hub Demo Script

## Goal
Close an owner/operator in one call by proving speed, control, and complete operational coverage.

## Opening (1 minute)
Use this framing:
- "This is your full shop operating system, not another billing tool."
- "You can run intake, work orders, inspections, estimates, invoices, and follow-up from one place."
- "It is sold as a one-time license so your cost does not keep climbing month after month."

## Demo Sequence (15 minutes)
1. Public booking
- Open the booking page.
- Create a sample appointment in under 60 seconds.
- Emphasize customer convenience and no front-desk bottleneck.

2. Front-desk intake
- Show appointment in the staff workflow.
- Confirm customer + vehicle details and create a work order.

3. Technician workflow
- Add labor lines and findings.
- Capture digital inspection details/photos.
- Highlight structured, repeatable process.

4. Estimate to invoice
- Generate an estimate.
- Convert to invoice.
- Show clean customer-ready output.

5. Business control
- Show inventory/parts impact.
- Show reporting snapshot and labor visibility.
- Reinforce that owners can see work and money flow at a glance.

## Objection Handling
- "We already use multiple tools"
  - "You can keep paying integration tax, or centralize and remove handoff failures."
- "We cannot afford another subscription"
  - "This is exactly why the model is perpetual license: predictable ownership cost."
- "Setup seems hard"
  - "We provide an onboarding checklist and can bundle migration support."

## Closing CTA
Use this close:
- "If this fits your workflow, we can lock in your launch package and start onboarding this week."
