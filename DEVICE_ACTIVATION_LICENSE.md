# Device Activation License Addendum

This Device Activation License Addendum ("Addendum") is entered into under and made part of the Master Software License Agreement between Licensor and Licensee (the "MSA"). If there is a conflict between this Addendum and the MSA, this Addendum controls only for device activation and entitlement terms.

## 1. Scope

1. This Addendum governs software activation tied to specific devices, device counts, and activation lifecycle events.
2. Capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings given in the MSA.

## 2. Device-Based Entitlement

1. A "Licensed Device" is one unique hardware endpoint identified by a device fingerprint, serial number, or Licensor-approved unique device identifier.
2. Licensee may activate the Software only on the number of Licensed Devices purchased in the applicable Order Form.
3. Each activation consumes one device entitlement unless otherwise stated in the Order Form.

## 3. Activation Mechanism

1. Software activation may require one or more of the following: license key, activation token, online verification, periodic revalidation, or signed offline activation file.
2. Licensor may issue separate activation profiles for production, staging, test, or disaster recovery environments.
3. Licensee must implement reasonable controls to protect activation keys and credentials from unauthorized access.

## 4. Rehost, Replacement, and Transfer

1. Licensee may rehost or transfer an activation from one device to another when hardware fails, is replaced, or is decommissioned.
2. Unless otherwise stated in the Order Form, Licensee may perform up to [X] self-service transfers per twelve (12) month period per purchased device license.
3. Transfers above this threshold may require Licensor approval and may incur an administrative fee.

## 5. Offline Activation

1. If internet access is unavailable, Licensor may provide offline activation files with a fixed validity period.
2. Licensee must renew or revalidate offline activations before expiration.
3. Licensee is responsible for secure handling of exported request files and returned license artifacts.

## 6. Deactivation and Revocation

1. Licensee must promptly deactivate licenses from retired, sold, lost, or compromised devices.
2. Licensor may suspend or revoke activations for material breach, non-payment, or misuse, subject to the MSA cure terms when applicable.
3. Emergency revocation may occur immediately where required to protect platform integrity, security, or legal compliance.

## 7. True-Up and Overuse

1. If active devices exceed purchased entitlements, Licensee must promptly purchase additional device licenses.
2. Licensor may invoice backdated fees for unauthorized overuse, prorated from the date overuse began (if reasonably determinable) or otherwise from first notice date.

## 8. Audit Data and Privacy

1. Activation telemetry may include device identifier, software version, activation timestamps, and license status.
2. Licensor will process activation telemetry in accordance with applicable law and Licensor's privacy policy.
3. Licensee will provide required notices to end users where law requires transparency for device-level telemetry.

## 9. Support and Availability

1. Activation service availability targets and support response times, if any, are defined in the Order Form or support policy.
2. Licensor is not responsible for activation failures caused by Licensee network restrictions, misconfiguration, unsupported hardware, or third-party outages.

## 10. Fees

1. Device activation fees, rehost fees, and overage rates are set out in the applicable Order Form.
2. Unless otherwise specified, all fees are non-refundable.

## 11. Term

This Addendum remains in effect for as long as Licensee uses any device-based activation model under the MSA.

## 12. Order Form Fields (Recommended)

Include the following in each Order Form:

- Product name and edition
- Purchased device count
- Price per device
- Overage pricing
- Self-service transfer allowance
- Offline activation term length
- Activation revalidation interval
- Environment scope (production/test/staging)
- Support tier and SLA
