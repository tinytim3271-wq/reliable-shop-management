# Pilot Success Milestones

## Week 0: Kickoff
- Intake form completed and approved.
- Pilot scope fixed (locations, users, migrated data).
- Go-live date confirmed.

## Week 1: Setup + Training
- Staff accounts and role permissions configured.
- Shop profile/hours and booking settings configured.
- Front-desk training completed.
- Technician workflow training completed.

## Week 2: Operational Activation
- First live customer booking received.
- First live work order created and completed.
- First estimate converted to paid invoice.
- At least one digital inspection completed.

## Week 3: Adoption + Stability
- Daily active use by front desk and tech team.
- Core workflows completed without external tools.
- Response time and reliability acceptable to owner.
- No open P1 defects in production workflow.

## Week 4: Value Realization
- Owner review meeting completed.
- Time-to-invoice and handoff friction reduced.
- Pilot testimonial request made.
- Expansion decision documented (continue/expand).

## Exit Criteria (Pilot Success)
- At least 80% of daily jobs processed in Reliable Shop Systems Hub.
- Team can execute booking -> work order -> estimate -> invoice without support.
- Owner confirms platform is replacing prior fragmented process.
